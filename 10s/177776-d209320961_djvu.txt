hereafter  referred  to  as  'Lessee',  are  parties  to  that  certain  Oil  and  Gas  Lease  dated  August  30,  2006,  and  recorded  as

F.G.  MuIIiken  Survey,  A-1045,  Tract  10

dated  August  30,  2006,  said  lands  are  now  fully  vested  with  Jeanne  B.  Godfrey,  as  Mr.  John  T.  Godfrey  is  no  longer 
living  and  the  property  was  community  property.

now  in  use  in  the  Assessor's  office  of  Tarrant  County,  Texas,  also  being  described  in  that  certain  Warranty 
Deed,  dated  September  1,  1967,  by  and  between  Mamie  Simms,  a  widow  as  Grantor  and  John  T.  Godfrey 
and  wife,  Jeanne  B.  Godfrey  as  Grantee,  recorded  in  Volume  4453,  Page  890,  Official  Public  Records, 
Tarrant  County,  Texas."  so  that  the  legal  description  now  reads: 

"0.300  Acres,  more  or  less,  out  of  the  F.G.  Mulliken  Survey,  Fort  Worth,  Tarrant  County,  Texas  and  designated  on
