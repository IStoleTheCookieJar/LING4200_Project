Introduction 


This directory consists of two major 
sections: Thematic and Geographic. 
only communities with incorporated 
Native organizations are listed. 
Individuals are not cited in the 
directory. Headings in the 
Geographic Section of the directory 
reflect the field areas of Native 
Community Branch offices. 


While listings have been updated and 
checked for accuracy, undoubtedly 
errors and omissions exist. Agencies 
should feel free to bring errors to the 
attention of the branch.
