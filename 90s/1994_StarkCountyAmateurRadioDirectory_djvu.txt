FOOTBALL HALL OF FAME CERTIFICATE 
This beautiful certificate issued by the Canton Amateur Radio 

Club to any Amateur Radio Operator in the world, that can verify they 
have contacted many members of the club. For all state side Amateurs, 
they must contact eleven (11) members and send in log data including; 
Date, Band, Mode and Call of CARC member worked. For all DX stations, 
including our neighbors in Canada, they have to work five (5) members 
of CARC and send in the same log data. 


Amateurs requesting this certificate, must send this info to the 
Certificate Custodian: Mickie Parks (XYL of K8JZN), 8307 Cleveland Ave 
NW, North Canton OH 44720. Please remember, the station in Stark 
County that is contacted, must be a member of the CARC. If you are not 
a member of CARC and wish to promote this certificate, contact any of 
the officers and they can supply you with the information on how to 
become a member. Let's get together and put Canton on the map, by 
plugging and promoting this certificate. 


CANTON AMATEUR RADIO CLUB 


On January 29th, 1941, a group of Canton area Amateurs met in the 


Post Office in downtown Canton, Ohio and formed what is now the Canton 
Amateur Radio Club. 


This was the first meeting, with Mr Paul Hughes W8UIL being unani- 
mously elected President of the newly formed Amateur Radio Club. Mr. 
Fred Hall was elected Secretary. At the second meeting, held on Feb- 
ruary 7th 1941, other officers were elected as follows: Mr. Martin 
(Babe) Hunker W8RQK Vice-President and Dr. Don Volzer W8SQK as Sec- 
Treasurer. They took up a collection and the treasury stood at $1.45. 


Charter members that are still members 53 years later are: Paul 
Hughes W8UIL, Belden Blake W8WNU and Joe Vignos ex-W8VAD, now W1FEZ.

Meetings for the Canton Amateur Radio Club are held every third 
Wednesday of the month at 7:30 PM at the Emergency Preparedness Center 
located in the Stark County Safety Building, located east of Canton at 
4500 Atlantic Blvd NE. Our club station is set up at that location for 
emergency communications, should they be needed. 


All are welcome to attend our meetings whether you are a member or 
not. Come out and join us and enjoy an eyeball with some of the Hams 
you have talked to on the air. You will be WELCOMED.
